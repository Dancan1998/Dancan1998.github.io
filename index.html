About myself

I am an Intelligent Science and Technology student which is a sub branch of Computer Science and Technology. I, personally, am very much attracted to technology and its core developments over time. The change of technology and all its wide application in the modern world is what got me into IT. I am a determined developer with the idea that I will leave a positive impact in the tech industry.

I find myself extremely drawn towards creating applications. To me this seems like an easy solution to many of today's problems. I have studied html,css,javascript,python,django web framework,java, assembly language for robotics design and the various frameworks associated with these. I have a better understanding of data structures, operating systems, computer security literacy,data mining and all these are equipping me to be a better programmer
